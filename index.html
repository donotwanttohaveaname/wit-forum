Everything you need to know before the big day.

The Forum is our flagship event. One full day where member companies, professionals and role models from across the Finnish tech community land in the same room for learning, networking and honest conversation.

We have built this edition around what you told us you wanted more of. Every career stage belongs here, from first-year tech professionals to senior leaders, with an emphasis on women.

Theme

Tech Horizons: Impacting our Future

Technology is reshaping industries, careers, and the society we all live in. Forum 2026 is where the community gets to look at that head on, and decide how we want to show up.

What to expect

Keynotes that stay with you

Bright minds sharing the trends, research and stories shaping the next chapter of tech. Leave with something new to think about on the way home.

Honest conversations

Panels and workshops where real people talk about real work. Practical, generous, and happy to sit with the uncomfortable questions.

Your new favourite people

Peers, mentors and role models from across the Finnish tech community, all in one room. Bring your questions and a full battery.
