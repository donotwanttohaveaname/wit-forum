The Forum

Everything you need to know before the big day.

The WiT Forum is our flagship event. One full day where member companies, professionals and role models from across the Finnish tech community land in the same room for learning, networking and honest conversation.

We have built this edition around what you told us you wanted more of. Every career stage belongs here, from first-year tech professionals to senior leaders, with an emphasis on women.

Theme

Tech Horizons: Impacting our Future

Technology is reshaping industries, careers, and the society we all live in. Forum 2026 is where the community gets to look at that head on, and decide how we want to show up.

Frequently asked

Answers, ahead of time.

Is the Forum free to attend?

The Forum is free for WiT community members and €49 for non-members.

Community membership is completely free for individuals. It gets you into every WiT event at no cost, plus a network of peers across Finnish tech and a steady stream of inspiration from the people shaping where the industry is going. Join the community and your Forum ticket is covered.

Who are the confirmed speakers and partners?

We reveal speakers and partners one by one in the months leading up to the Forum. It keeps the lineup exciting and gives every name the spotlight they deserve.

Drop your email in the sign-up and you will get every announcement the moment it goes out. You can also follow Women in Tech Finland on LinkedIn and Instagram.

Who is the Forum for?

Every career stage in tech, from first-year students to senior executives, with an emphasis on women. If you work in tech or are curious about working in tech, you belong in this room.

Is there a dress code?

No dress code. Wear something you feel confident and comfortable in. Company-branded gear is welcome if that is your thing.

Is the Forum live-streamed?

Yes. The stage programme is live-streamed so the community can tune in from anywhere.
